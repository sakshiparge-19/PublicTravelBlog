Introduction

Manali, nestled in the Himalayas, is a paradise for outdoor adventurers. This charming town offers a wide array of thrilling activities, from white-water rafting and paragliding to trekking and skiing. With its stunning natural beauty, including snow-capped peaks and gushing rivers, Manali provides an unforgettable experience for adrenaline enthusiasts and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

Travel Tips and Hacks

  • Best time to visit: May to June and September to October for trekking, rafting; December to February for snow sports.
  • Pack smart: Warm clothing, including thermals and waterproof jackets.
  • Book in advance: Accommodation and popular activities fill up quickly.
  • Stay hydrated, acclimatize gradually, and beware of altitude sickness.
  • Always opt for certified operators and guides.
  • Respect the environment: Avoid littering, stay on designated trails.
  • Cash is king: ATMs can be scarce, so carry enough cash.
  • Stay informed: Check local news and forecasts.

Outdoor Adventures

  • Trekking: Hampta Pass, Bhrigu Lake, and Beas Kund treks.
  • River Rafting: Beas River rafting, especially between Pirdi and Jhiri.
  • Paragliding: Fly in Solang Valley or Marhi.
  • Camping: Solang Valley, Sethan, Bhuntar.
  • Skiing & Snowboarding: Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass.
  • Other Activities: Zorbing, rock climbing, rappelling, biking, yak rides.
